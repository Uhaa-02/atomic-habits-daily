## Chapter Summary
In this chapter, James Clear explains how **tracking habits** and maintaining awareness help sustain long-term behavior change. Seeing progress creates motivation and makes habits more satisfying.

## Key Ideas
- Habit tracking reinforces consistency.
- Visual progress motivates continued effort.
- Awareness prevents habits from fading over time.
- Small wins encourage long-term commitment.

## Key Takeaways
- Track habits to stay accountable.
- Progress is motivating, even when small.
- Systems help maintain consistency.
- Missing once is not failure; repeating the miss is.

## Personal Reflection
This chapter helped me understand the importance of tracking habits. Seeing progress visually motivates me to continue even on days when motivation is low.
