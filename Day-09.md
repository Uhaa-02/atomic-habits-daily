## Key Ideas
- Self-control is about environment, not willpower.
- The best way to break bad habits is to avoid triggers.

## Key Takeaways
- Disciplined people structure their lives to minimize temptation.
- Willpower is limited; environment is constant.
- Make bad habits invisible to reduce their influence.
- Change surroundings before trying to change behavior.
- Bad habits are autocatalytic: the process feeds itself.
- Rather than making it obvious, make it invisible.
